Equipment Desk App Solution

client:
Breda University of Applied Sciences

This project was the final delivery of the PRJ1 - Design Thinking course. PRJ1 is designed to teach students about user-centred design. Within the project, students are given the freedom to choose any issue that students of the university face. They are then tasked to create a low-fidelity app solution to the chosen problem based on research.
